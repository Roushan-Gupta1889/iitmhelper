‚ö†Ô∏è MAJOR GRADING POLICY CHANGE - January 2026

Important changes to the Graded Assignment Assessment (GAA) weightage have been announced for the January 2026 term. Students should carefully review these changes as they significantly impact evaluation patterns.

Key Changes:

  • Foundation Level Courses: GAA weightage is now 0 marks. Questions for a total of 10 marks in the 2 quizzes and End term exam will come from the assignments.
  • Diploma Level Courses: GAA weightage is now 5 marks. Questions for a total of 5 marks in the 2 quizzes and End term exam will come from the assignments.
  • Degree Level Courses: GAA weightage remains unchanged.
  • Important: Check every course grading pattern to see actual weightages as they may vary.

What This Means:

  • Foundation level students: Focus on understanding concepts rather than just completing assignments
  • Diploma level students: Assignments still contribute but with reduced direct weightage
  • All students: Review individual course grading patterns carefully
  • Exam questions will still be based on assignment topics, but weightage has changed

April 2024 Program Info

üìñ Program Structure Overview

The IIT Madras BS in Data Science and Applications is a comprehensive 4-year online degree program with flexible completion options.

Program Levels:

  • Foundation Level: 32 credits (8 courses) - Mathematics, Statistics, Programming, English
  • Diploma Level: 54 credits total
    • Diploma in Programming: 27 credits (6 courses + 2 projects)
    • Diploma in Data Science: 27 credits (6 courses + 2 projects)
  • Degree Level: 56 credits - Core courses + Electives

Key Features:

  • Flexible completion: 3-6 years
  • Exit options at Diploma level
  • 36 theory courses + 4 project courses
  • Tri-semester system (January, May, September)
  • Suggested pace: 10 hours/course/week
